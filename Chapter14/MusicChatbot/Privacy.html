Personal info

Music Chatbot doesn't collect personally identifiable information. We do use the input collected only to improve the service. Music Chatbot uses 3rd party services that may also use the data to improve their service.

Children

Music Chatbot is recommended for persons who are 18 and older.

How we collect info

Music Chatbot may use 3rd party Natural Language Processing (NLP) services that help recognize a user text and use the information in the text to figure out how to answer.

Our Music Chatbot bot is enabled by Microsoft Bot Framework. The Microsoft Bot Framework is a set of web-services that enable intelligent services and connections using conversation channels you authorize. As a service provider, Microsoft will transmit content you provide to our bot/service in order to enable the service. For more information about Microsoft privacy policies please see their privacy statement here: http://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?LinkId=521839. In addition, your interactions with this bot/service are also subject to the conversational channel's applicable terms of use, privacy and data collection policies. To report abuse when using a bot that uses the Microsoft Bot Framework to Microsoft, please visit the Microsoft Bot Framework website at https://www.botframework.com and use the “Report Abuse” link in the menu to contact Microsoft.

Tracking technologies

Music Chatbot doesn't use tracking technologies. Music Chatbot does not collect location information. However, 3rd party platforms might use tracking and you can consult the privacy policy of the platform or messaging system that you use Music Chatbot with.

Any information you provide is used to improve Music Chatbot's performance. e.g. if Music Chatbot doesn't recognize a question, we'll examine the question and use 3rd party tools or custom code in trying to provide a better answer to those types of questions in the future.
